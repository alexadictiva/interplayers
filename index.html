Nosotros

Founded in 2000, SEG Football has been leaving its mark in the industry. With 300+ clients and 2,000+ deals and contract extensions, we have built a reputation of reliability and integrity in the football world.

SEG Football deals with all the elite clubs in Europe, but is also active on a local level, which is why many talented prospects choose for our services. We make the difference with our unique way of operating, focused on long term career development and maximising talent development.

Transparent and skilled, our staff is in 25+ countries, gathering and combining market information from all around the globe. SEG Football's one and only goal: to help our clients maximise their talent.
